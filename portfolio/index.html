My Design thinking

UX Design is not just a way to design an application and through in some components or just making things look pretty. It is about understanding the users emotion, they should not find understanding and using an application a burden or should not spend day to get trained. I know there is nothing called perfection but in UX world the closest thing to perfect design is aethetics with ease in the usage for everyone.
